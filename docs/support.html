Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add widgets?

Long-press your Home Screen or Lock Screen, tap the "+" button, search for "StandBy Hub", and choose the widget you want. For StandBy mode, add widgets through the StandBy widget picker on iOS 17+.

Widgets are not updating

iOS manages widget refresh schedules. Try these steps:

  • Open the StandBy Hub app to trigger a refresh
  • Remove and re-add the widget
  • Restart your device

Weather widget shows no data

The Weather widget requires location access. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > StandBy Hub and select "While Using the App" or "Always".

Calendar widget shows no events

The Calendar widget requires calendar access. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Calendars > StandBy Hub and enable access.

What is StandBy Hub Pro?

Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks all clock styles, premium ambient backgrounds, custom accent colors, and additional dashboard slots. No subscription — pay once, keep forever.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open StandBy Hub, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • StandBy mode requires iPhone (not available on iPad)
  • Home Screen widgets work on both iPhone and iPad

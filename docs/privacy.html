STANDBY HUB

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

StandBy Hub is a widget app developed by tmmr. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data practices.

Data Collection

StandBy Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data (widget configurations, settings, dashboard layouts) is stored locally on your device only.

Device Permissions

StandBy Hub may request the following permissions to provide widget functionality:

  • Calendar — Used by the Calendar widget to display your upcoming events. Calendar data is read locally and never leaves your device.
  • Location — Used by the Weather widget to show local weather conditions. Location data is processed locally and not shared with third parties.

These permissions are optional. You can use the app without granting them — only the corresponding widgets will be unavailable.

Advertising

StandBy Hub does not display any advertisements.

In-App Purchases

StandBy Hub offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store). We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple — In-App Purchases, WidgetKit

Children's Privacy

StandBy Hub does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
